Responsible Gaming

Guidelines for Safe Play

  • Set a budget and stick to it
  • Treat gaming as entertainment, not a way to make money
  • Take regular breaks from playing
  • Don't chase losses
  • Be aware of the time you spend gaming
  • Never gamble under the influence of alcohol or drugs
  • Balance gaming with other activities

Resources

  • BeGambleAware
  • Gamblers Anonymous
  • National Council on Problem Gambling

Self-Assessment

If you're concerned about your gaming habits, ask yourself these questions:

  1. Do you spend more time or money on gaming than you intend to?
  2. Do you feel guilty or anxious about your gaming habits?
  3. Have you tried to cut back on gaming but couldn't?
  4. Has gaming negatively impacted your relationships or work?
  5. Do you think about gaming even when you're not playing?

If you answered "yes" to any of these questions, consider reaching out to one of the resources listed above for support.
